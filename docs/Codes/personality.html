Satoru Gojo, a fictional character from the popular manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen", is my chosen hero. He exudes an unwavering self-assurance that's both admirable and infectious. His confident demeanor inspires those around him, particularly his students.

PERSONALITY ASSESSMENT

Reflection on Personality Test Results

After taking the Keirsey Temperament Sorter, I found the results to be both insightful and thought-provoking. The test identified me as an "Guardian" personality type, characterized by duty, humbleness, and hardworking.

While I believe this assessment captures some key aspects of my personality, particularly my desire to provide for my loves ones and responsible nature, I also recognize the limitations of such tests. Some questions didn't really apply to me.

I see this assessment as a starting point for self-reflection rather than a definitive label. It highlighted areas where I can leverage my strengths, such as my ability to see the bigger picture, and areas where I can grow, like growing my social skills.

Moving forward, I plan to use these insights to inform my personal development goals, while remaining open to different experiences and challenges that will continue to shape my character both academically and personally.
